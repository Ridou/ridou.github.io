Advancing the Advancements in Technology

Tech Issues

There is a constant issue in technology that will phase through time and time. There has always been the pattern that good hardware has to be created in order to advance the future of technology, but the creation of new hardware has to come from an idea. There have been many ideas that have come to life because they were first in a sci-fi movie although the director of these movies does not get the credit they deserve for giving the engineers the idea.

Since when the wheel was made and made travel faster, to an iPhone that made it more convenient to connect people together with the usage of apps. It does not always have to be a hardware solution for technology to advance but the way we use our tools. Various inventors are hackers, which sadly is being used as a taboo but find a way to use the tool in a different but more efficient matter. For instance you have the man the created power, but then you have the man the created solar power. They are big difference here, but which source of energy is more sustainable through time? They are both great inventions and will be used, but the point is that humanity has to and will continue to hack life to find a better way to live in this world. This to me is the thought of living in paradise and our inventors are what are making the path for us.

Most future advancements will change the way we use technology. Just by walking down the streets of San Francisco, these days you can see children to the elderly communicating with their devices with people all around the world where they would before have to send telegrams or go tot he market to find that person you always see there. This part of technology has connected people socially more than people could imagine that now they believe it has replaced social behavior, but I disagree. This is a different social behavior and there is still a behavior present, just because it is a different way to communicate does not mean that it is the incorrect way to do it. This is only how technology has changed the social aspect of our daily lives in the world. There will be future advancements that will advance our way of travel(airplanes, hover boards), education(global community, augmented reality), food(automated community farming), music(synthetic frequencies). The amazing part is that this could all be accessible to everyone by using their smart device. The human will create a dependency to a smart device that will do all these functions for us and I do not see it as a weakness but as an external upgrade(hard drive) to store all our advances in technology in a man-made universe.


-Richard Santin
